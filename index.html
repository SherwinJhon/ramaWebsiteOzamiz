Welcome to Ozamiz

Ozamiz City, located in Misamis Occidental in northern Mindanao, Philippines, is often called the "Gem of Panguil Bay" for its natural beauty and strategic location. Surrounded by lush mountains and pristine coastal areas, the city is a perfect blend of history, culture, and nature. A prominent landmark is Fort Santiago, or Cotta Fort, a historic Spanish fort that reflects its colonial past. Beside it stands the Birhen sa Cotta Shrine, a religious site popular among Catholic devotees. For adventurers, the nearby Malindang Mountain Range offers trekking, wildlife exploration, and breathtaking views.

Culturally rich, Ozamiz City celebrates the Subayan Keg Subanon Festival, showcasing the vibrant traditions of the Subanon tribe through music and dance. The city is a bustling center of trade and commerce, leveraging its location along Panguil Bay for transportation and economic activities. Known for its warm and welcoming locals, Ozamiz City is a destination that offers a mix of history, adventure, and community charm, making it a must-visit in the Philippines.

Mission

To be a dynamic and progressive city that seamlessly blends its rich historical heritage, breathtaking natural beauty, and innovative development. Ozamiz envisions a future where all residents enjoy a high quality of life, opportunities for growth are accessible to everyone, and visitors are drawn to its vibrant culture, sustainable practices, and welcoming community.

Vision

Our mission is to preserve and celebrate the cultural and historical identity of Ozamiz while fostering sustainable growth and development. We aim to provide an inclusive and supportive environment where residents can thrive through access to education, livelihood, and essential services. We are dedicated to promoting tourism by showcasing our natural and historical treasures, fostering economic opportunities, and ensuring that all advancements respect and protect the environment. By working together as one community, we strive to build a city that inspires pride and progress for current and future generations.

Core Values

We uphold integrity, respect, and inclusivity in all our actions. We value unity and collaboration, fostering a spirit of community and care. We are committed to sustainability, protecting our environment while promoting progress that benefits everyone equally.
